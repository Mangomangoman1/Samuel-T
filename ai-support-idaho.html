FAQ

Straight answers about AI support.

What kind of AI support does Actrix offer?

Actrix offers ongoing AI support for Idaho businesses: fixing broken or misconfigured AI tools, installing new systems, training staff to use AI effectively, optimizing workflows, maintaining custom agent systems, and troubleshooting when something stops working. Support is available by phone, text, or remote session.

Do I need a maintenance contract for AI support?

Not necessarily. Actrix offers both one-time support sessions for specific issues and ongoing support plans for businesses that want regular maintenance, monitoring, and optimization. You can start with a single fix and expand if needed.

What if I already bought AI tools but nobody on my team uses them?

That is one of the most common support requests Actrix receives. Typical issues: the tool was never set up for your actual workflow, staff did not get useful training, or the tool does not integrate with the tools you already use. Actrix can audit the situation, reconfigure the tools, and train the team.

Can Actrix fix an AI setup that someone else built?

Yes. Actrix regularly audits and fixes AI systems built by other consultants, freelancers, or the business owner. Common problems: broken automation workflows, mismatched tools, poor prompt design, security gaps, and systems that stop working after updates.

Is AI support available remotely?

Support is available remotely for businesses across Idaho. Most fixes, training sessions, and maintenance happen over a remote connection or by phone. For owners in the Hailey, Ketchum, and Sun Valley area, in-person support may also be available.

How fast is support turnaround for urgent issues?

Urgent support requests — broken automation, down agent systems, or critical workflow failures — are typically addressed within 24 hours. Routine support and training sessions are scheduled within 2-5 business days depending on current workload.
