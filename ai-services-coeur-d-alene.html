FAQ

Straight answers for North Idaho business owners.

Is Actrix based in Coeur d'Alene?

Actrix is based in Hailey, Idaho and serves businesses across the state. We do not have a Coeur d'Alene storefront, but we work remotely with North Idaho businesses through text, phone, video calls, and onsite visits when needed. We are honest about being a statewide firm — not pretending to be local to every city.

Does the AI handle seasonal volume without breaking?

Yes. The system scales with your seasonal needs — handling peak summer volume the same way it handles quiet shoulder seasons. The agent works the same hours you do, and you can adjust intake rules, availability windows, and response tone as the season changes.

What if I need hands-on help setting things up?

Most of our setup is done remotely: we build the agent, test with real scenarios, and walk you through the workflow over a video call. For North Idaho businesses that want an in-person session, we can arrange onsite visits for setup, training, or troubleshooting.

Can this work with my existing software?

Usually yes. We map the integration during setup so captured leads and activity flow into the tools you already use — CRMs, calendar apps, email, project management platforms, and existing AI subscriptions.

How are you different from the big AI consulting firms?

We build one business at a time. No sales pipeline, no junior associates, no generic slide decks. We do the build ourselves, you approve every message, and we stay on the line after launch. Big firms sell process; we sell a working system.

How much does it cost for a Coeur d'Alene business?

Starter AI agents typically range from $750–$3,000 depending on complexity. We custom-build to your specific workflow, so the price depends on what the agent needs to handle. Text us your biggest bottleneck and we can give you a real estimate within a day.

Yes. This page covers the entire North Idaho region — Coeur d'Alene, Post Falls, Hayden, Rathdrum, and the surrounding Kootenai County area. The same service model applies: custom AI agents, automation, support, and training for any business in the area.

How fast can I get started?
