Common requests

What Pocatello businesses are asking us to build.

These are the most common requests we hear from Southeast Idaho business owners. Each one maps to a specific AI agent that collects information, prepares work, and hands decisions back to the owner.

01

Shift scheduling assistant

Employees text about shift swaps, coverage gaps, and schedule changes. The agent checks availability, applies rules, proposes solutions, and sends confirmed updates — all without a supervisor typing a single message. Particularly valuable for 24/7 manufacturing and healthcare operations.

02

Patient & client intake

A new patient calls after hours or submits a web form. The agent responds immediately, collects insurance details, preferred times, and reason for visit, and builds an intake-ready brief. Front desk starts the morning with a full queue instead of an empty voicemail box.

03

Supplier & inventory coordination

The agent tracks open orders, follows up with suppliers, logs status changes, and flags items that need human attention. Manufacturing and retail operations in Southeast Idaho use this to keep the supply chain visible without adding procurement headcount.

04

Staff AI training

Your team is already using ChatGPT — inconsistently, unsafely, and without knowing what not to do. We train them on prompts, privacy, approval rules, and where automation actually saves time. Especially important for teams spread across shifts who need consistent AI practices.

FAQ

Straight answers for Southeast Idaho business owners.

Is Actrix based in Pocatello?

Actrix is based in Hailey, Idaho and serves businesses across the state. We do not have a Pocatello storefront, but we work remotely with Southeast Idaho businesses through text, phone, video calls, and onsite visits when needed. We are honest about being a statewide firm — not pretending to be local to every city.

Does the AI handle multi-shift operations?

Yes. The agent works across all shifts — answering routine questions, logging maintenance tickets, drafting shift reports, and escalating safety or production issues to the right supervisor regardless of the hour. Night-shift workers get the same response quality as day shift.

What if we need hands-on help setting things up?

Most of our setup is done remotely: we build the agent, test with real scenarios, and walk you through the workflow over a video call. For Southeast Idaho businesses that want an in-person session, we can arrange onsite visits for setup, training, or troubleshooting.

Can this work with my existing manufacturing or healthcare software?

Usually yes. We map the integration during setup so captured leads and activity flow into the tools you already use — scheduling platforms, EHR/EMR systems, CRMs, email, project management platforms, and existing AI subscriptions.

How are you different from big AI consulting firms?

We build one business at a time. No sales pipeline, no junior associates, no generic slide decks. We do the build ourselves, you approve every message, and we stay on the line after launch. Big firms sell process; we sell a working system.

How much does it cost for a Pocatello business?

Starter AI agents typically range from $750–$3,000 depending on complexity. We custom-build to your specific workflow, so the price depends on what the agent needs to handle. Text us your biggest bottleneck and we can give you a real estimate within a day.

Do you also serve Chubbuck, Blackfoot, and American Falls?

Yes. This page covers the entire Southeast Idaho region — Pocatello, Chubbuck, Blackfoot, American Falls, McCammon, and the surrounding Bannock and Bingham County areas. The same service model applies: custom AI agents, automation, support, and training for any business in the area.

How fast can I get started?

Most businesses are live within one week of the first conversation. The setup call takes about 45 minutes. We build your workflows, test with real scenarios, and launch with a soft go-live before full deployment.
