How it works

Three steps from missed call to owner-ready brief.

The agent does not pretend to be you. It collects the facts, organizes them, and hands you something you can act on in sixty seconds.

The call lands on the agent

When you miss a call, the customer gets a fast text back instead of silence. The agent introduces itself, explains you are on site, and asks what the customer needs.

Agent gathers the details

Job type, size, location, timeline, budget range, and photos. The agent works the conversation like a good apprentice — thorough, friendly, and never pushy.

You review, price, and send

A clean brief lands in your phone. You check the details, add pricing, and approve. The quote goes out. The customer gets a warm, professional experience — even though you were on a ladder.

FAQ

Straight answers for contractors.

Does the agent answer calls or just text?

It texts the caller back immediately with a friendly message and starts a conversation. It does not pretend to be you on the phone. The goal is fast response and clean information, not a robot voice reading a script.

What happens if the customer has a complex question?

The agent handles common questions (availability, service area, rough pricing ranges, timeline estimates). For anything complex or custom, it says “Let me get the owner — they will have the best answer” and routes it to you as a priority note.

Do I need to install anything or learn software?

No. Everything works through text messages and a simple review link. If you can send a text, you can use the system. No app store, no login, no training.

What kind of contractors does this work for?

General contractors, electricians, plumbers, HVAC, landscapers, painters, roofers, foundation contractors, excavation, concrete, and handyman services. If you go to a job site and miss calls while you work, this is built for you.

How fast can I get this running?

Most contractors are up and running within a week of the first conversation. We tailor the scripts to your trade, your service area, and the questions you actually get asked.

What does “custom AI agent” mean compared to a chatbot?

A chatbot answers questions from a script. A custom agent collects information, makes judgments about what matters, organizes the results, and presents them for your approval before anything goes out. It learns your business context over time.
