Questions owners usually ask

Plain answers before anything gets automated.

Is this just ChatGPT for my business?

No. ChatGPT can be part of the stack, but the real work is the system around it: intake rules, prompts, approvals, data sources, handoffs, templates, and fallback behavior.

Can we start small?

Yes. That is the recommended path. Start with one repeated workflow: missed-call rescue, quote intake, review requests, customer FAQs, or follow-up reminders.

Will AI talk to customers without me approving it?

Not by default. For anything involving money, scheduling, safety, sensitive customer info, or brand trust, the safer first version drafts for human review.

What if my team is not technical?

That is normal. The system should meet the business where it already works: email, texts, forms, spreadsheets, shared docs, calendars, or lightweight dashboards.

What businesses is this for?

Contractors, restaurants, property managers, clinics, salons, service shops, retailers, repair shops, and professional services — especially businesses where the owner is still doing too much admin by hand.

Do I need to change all my software?

Usually no. The first move is mapping the current workflow and building around it. If a tool is blocking the system, then a change can be discussed — not assumed.
