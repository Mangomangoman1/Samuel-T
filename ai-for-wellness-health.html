How it works

Three steps from missed call to confirmed booking.

The agent does not pretend to be you. It collects what matters, organizes the intake, and hands you a client ready to schedule — without you touching a phone during a session.

When you miss a call, the customer gets a fast, warm text back instead of silence. The agent introduces itself, says you are with a client, and asks what they need.

FAQ

Straight answers for wellness professionals.

Does the agent answer calls or just text?

It texts the caller back immediately with a warm, human-sounding message and starts a conversation. It does not pretend to be you on the phone. The goal is fast response and clean information, not a robot voice.

Can it handle sensitive health information?

The agent collects basic intake details (service type, timing, preferences) that clients share freely by text. For anything more sensitive — health history details, insurance, or medical forms — the agent sends a secure link or says “I will have the owner follow up on that” and routes it to you. You decide the boundaries.

Do I need to install anything or learn software?

No. Everything works through text messages and a simple review link. If you can send a text, you can use the system. No app store, no login, no training. Your clients do not need to install anything either.

What kind of wellness businesses does this work for?

Massage therapists, chiropractors, physical therapists, estheticians, acupuncturists, yoga studios, med spas, wellness clinics, nutritionists, and any practice where the practitioner is hands-on during business hours.

Does this replace my front desk staff?

No. The best first use is catching what falls through the cracks: missed calls during sessions, intake paperwork that eats treatment time, and follow-ups that slip your mind. If you have front desk staff, this gives them better-prepared leads. If you do not, it fills the gap.

How fast can I get this running?

Most practices are up and running within a week of the first conversation. We tailor the scripts to your services, your hours, your intake requirements, and the questions your clients actually ask.
