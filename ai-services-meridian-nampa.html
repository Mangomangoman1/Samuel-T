Rapid growth creates operational strain: more leads, more customers, more follow-up, and more admin work — often before you have hired the staff to handle it. An AI agent absorbs that volume immediately. It handles inbound inquiries, qualifies leads, schedules follow-ups, processes orders, and hands ready-to-act-on briefs to your team.
