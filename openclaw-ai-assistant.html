Compelling use cases

One AI assistant across the channels where work already happens.

OpenClaw makes an AI assistant reachable without forcing staff to adopt a new tool. The assistant comes to them.

One assistant across channels

Telegram, Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, Signal, iMessage, Google Chat, Microsoft Teams, Matrix, and more can route through one gateway.

Local-first control

You run the gateway on your machine or server, keeping setup, routing, sessions, and access rules under your control instead of handing everything to a cloud provider.

Works with any AI backend

Why it matters

AI that lives where people already talk gets used more.

The hardest part of introducing AI into a business is not the technology. It is getting people to open another app. OpenClaw solves that by being the bridge.

  • Staff message the assistant from the tool they already use.
  • No new login, no new tab, no new habit to build.
  • Self-hosted means business data stays in-house.
  • Works with any AI model or agent backend.

Samuel's angle

"Put the assistant where the owner already checks messages."

For most small businesses, that is text messages, Signal with the team, or Slack if they are more organized. OpenClaw makes it possible to have one AI assistant appear in all of them at once — so nobody has to change how they communicate.
